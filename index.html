Important notes:

Minimum Funds Transaction:

The minimum amount required for participation in the Pre-Order is:

BTC 0,01 OR
ETH 0,2 OR
LTC 0,8

Transaction of funds lower than the indicated above will be returned to the payee after the end of token distribution period.

Funds storage

Your Pre-order funds will be stored in the currency you used for contribution. On the ICO Date your funds will be converted according to this process:

  1. Contributed currency will be converted to Euro.
  2. Euro will be converted to FND.

The exchange rate of Contributed Currency to Euro will be set according to the publicly open information from top European exchanges prior 6 hours before the distribution launch.

The exchange rate of the FND to EUR will be equal to 1.00 during the ICO Period. So 1 FND = 1 EUR
